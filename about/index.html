About the Author:

MICHAEL DUDLEY was born in the city of Toronto; for 35 years he lived and worked in rural Southwestern Ontario; since June, 2018 he has been continuously travelling. His writing has been internationally published in newspapers, magazines, journals, and anthologies. He is a member of Haiku Canada and the Haiku Society of America, the author of numerous poetry volumes, and the father of three children.
